## Become a Maintainer

Active contributors may be invited to become maintainers with:
- Direct merge access to main branch
- Input on roadmap decisions
- Access to private development discussions
- Early access to premium features

**Requirements:**
- At least 5 merged PRs
- Consistent code quality
- Community engagement (reviewing PRs, helping issues)
- Shared vision for RuleWise
